About me

Based in Canada

I'm Iryna Buchak, a brand designer based in Canada, specializing in Brand Strategy, Identity Design, and Creative Direction.

I believe that building a brand is about creating a genuine connection between a business and the people it serves. Thoughtful strategy ensures that every element has purpose and clarity — while refined design translates that strategy into a strong, cohesive visual language.

I work with startups and entrepreneurs to create branding that is clear, modern, and consistent. Whether you're launching a new business or refreshing an existing one, every project is built to communicate value, strengthen recognition, and support long-term growth.
